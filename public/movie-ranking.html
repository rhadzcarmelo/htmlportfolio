My Top 3 Movies of all Time

Howl's Moving Castle

The movie's message of love and kindness resonates with audiences of all ages, making it one of the best films created by Studio Ghibli.
Unlike its predecessors, Howl's Moving Castle is a love story. It is the story of Howl and Sophie falling in love and learning to accept themselves throughout the process.

Spirited Away

Chihiro's epic journey through the spirit world teaches many important life lessons, encouraging its viewers to never give up and face their greatest fears head-on.

My Neighbor Totoro

Along with its celebration of friendship, the major theme of My Neighbor Totoro is the beauty of nature. The film portrays the enchanting sceneries of rural
Japan through the lush forests, rolling hills, and clear streams. The natural world in the anime is a source of wonder where magic and reality coexist.
